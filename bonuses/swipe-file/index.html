15 Outreach Scripts That Get Responses

Copy, paste, and customize. These scripts are proven to get replies from local business owners.

Script 1 — Instagram DM (Cold)

The Simple Compliment Opener

Hey [Name] — love what you've done with [specific thing about their business]. Quick question — are you currently doing anything to bring in new customers online? I help local businesses in [your city] get more visibility. Happy to share what's working if it'd be useful.
Script 2 — Email (Cold)

The Spotted Something Opener

Subject: Quick question about [Business Name] Hi [Name], I was searching for [their type of business] in [city] and came across [Business Name]. Impressive reviews — looks like you're doing a lot right. I noticed you might not be showing up on the first page of Google for a couple of key searches. That could be leaving some customers on the table. I help local businesses fix exactly that. Would it make sense to jump on a quick 10-minute call to see if I can help? [Your name]
Script 3 — Facebook DM (Cold)

The Local Community Angle

Hi [Name], I'm a local marketer here in [city] and I've been following [Business Name] — you guys always show up at the community events. I help local businesses like yours get more customers through social media and Google. Not sure if it's something you'd be open to exploring, but happy to chat if so. No pressure at all.
Script 4 — Text Message (Cold)

The Short and Direct

Hi [Name], this is [Your name] — local marketing consultant in [city]. I help businesses like [Business Name] get more customers online. Would you be open to a quick 10-minute call this week?
Script 5 — LinkedIn (Cold)

The Professional Opener

Hi [Name] — I came across your profile while looking at local businesses in [city]. I specialize in helping [their industry] owners attract more customers through digital marketing. Would love to connect and see if there's any way I can add value. No pitch — just a quick conversation.
Script 6 — Follow-Up #1 (Day 3)

The Gentle Bump

Hey [Name] — just wanted to follow up on my message from a few days ago. Totally understand if now isn't the right time. If you're ever curious about what's working for other [their industry] businesses in [city], I'm happy to share. No strings attached.
Script 7 — Follow-Up #2 (Day 5)

The Value Add

Hi [Name] — one last follow-up and then I'll leave you alone, I promise. I put together a quick list of 3 things [their industry] businesses in [city] are doing right now to get more customers. Happy to send it over if you'd like — completely free, no catch.
Script 8 — Follow-Up #3 (Day 7)

The Graceful Exit

Hey [Name] — I'll stop reaching out after this. If the timing ever feels right to explore how to get more customers for [Business Name], feel free to reach back out. Wishing you a great rest of the week.
Script 9 — After Discovery Call (No Response)

The Post-Call Follow-Up

Hi [Name] — it was great speaking with you earlier this week. I put together a quick proposal based on what we discussed. Just wanted to check in and see if you had any questions before I send it over. Let me know what works!
Script 10 — After Proposal Sent

The Proposal Follow-Up (48 Hours)

Hi [Name] — just checking in on the proposal I sent over. Happy to answer any questions or adjust anything to make it work better for you. Let me know your thoughts when you get a chance!
Script 11 — Voicemail #1

The Short Intro

Hi [Name], this is [Your name] calling about [Business Name]. I'm a local marketing consultant here in [city] and I had a quick idea I wanted to share with you about getting more customers online. I'll send you a quick text as well. My number is [your number]. Thanks!
Script 12 — Voicemail #2

The Specific Insight

Hi [Name], [Your name] again. I was looking at your Google Business profile and noticed something that might be costing you customers. Takes about 10 minutes to fix. Would love to share it with you — no charge. Give me a call back at [your number] when you get a chance. Thanks!
Script 13 — Referral Introduction

When a Mutual Contact Referred You

Hi [Name], my name is [Your name] and [Referral name] suggested I reach out. I help local businesses in [city] get more customers and [Referral name] thought we might be a good fit to talk. Would love to set up a quick 10-minute call — my number is [your number]. Looking forward to connecting!
Script 14 — After Meeting In Person

The In-Person Follow-Up

Hey [Name], great meeting you at [event/location] today. This is [Your name]. I'd love to follow up on our conversation about helping [Business Name] get more customers online. How does your schedule look this week for a quick call?
Script 15 — Re-Engagement (Past Lead)

Reaching Out to an Old Prospect

Hi [Name], this is [Your name] — we spoke a few months back about marketing for [Business Name]. I know the timing wasn't right then, but I've been working with a few [their industry] owners in [city] recently with great results. Wanted to check in and see if things had changed. Would love to reconnect if so!
