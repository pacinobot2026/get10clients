The Proposal That Closes

A simple one-page proposal template that makes saying yes easy - plus how to price your services so you never undercharge again.

Video coming soon

Key Takeaways

  • One page maximum - long proposals create doubt, not confidence
  • Lead with the problem they described on the call, not your services
  • Price in packages (Good/Better/Best) to anchor the middle option
  • Include a clear start date to create urgency without pressure
  • Follow up on proposals exactly 48 hours after sending - silence is not a no
